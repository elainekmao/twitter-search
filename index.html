Welcome to Twitter Search! To start searching, enter a query in the search box.

You can search by just keywords, or you can add these operators to your search for more specific results!

  • place:247f43d441defc03 (This finds tweets about the place with Twitter ID 247f43d441defc03)
  • since:2013-09-15 (This finds tweets about your keywords that were posted after 2013-09-15)
  • until:2013-09-17 (This finds tweets about your keywords that were posted before 2013-09-15)

To avoid errors, please make sure you enter dates in the correct format!

Also, always make sure to have at least one keyword that is not a special operator.
